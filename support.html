Support and Advocacy for food truck businesses

Here are a list of links for food truck owners or aspiring owners who are seeking support from others in the industry. There are also links for owners who are social justice-focused or simply interested in giving back to their communities. Finally, it contains links for those interested in advocating for the needs/rights of food truck owners.

The Philadelphia Healthy Food Truck Initiative
  • The Philadelphia Healthy Food Truck Initiative is a program aimed at promoting healthier dining options for customers in the city. Operating through food trucks, this initiative seeks to enhance the accessibility of nutritious meals for residents and visitors. By collaborating with food truck vendors, the program encourages the inclusion of healthier ingredients and cooking methods in their offerings. The ultimate goal is to contribute to improved dietary choices within the community, fostering a culture of wellness and making it easier for people to make nutritious food choices while enjoying the convenience and diversity of food trucks.

The Philly Mobile Food Association
  • The Philly Mobile Food Association is a collective organization that represents and supports the vibrant mobile food industry in Philadelphia. Comprising a diverse group of food truck owners, operators, and enthusiasts, the association serves as a platform for collaboration, advocacy, and information exchange. Through its initiatives, the Philly Mobile Food Association works to enhance the visibility, professionalism, and success of food trucks throughout the city. It fosters a sense of community among its members, provides resources and guidance for navigating industry challenges, and organizes events to showcase the culinary talents and offerings of its mobile food members. Whether you're a food truck entrepreneur, a foodie, or someone interested in the mobile food scene, the Philly Mobile Food Association plays a vital role in shaping and promoting this dynamic aspect of Philadelphia's culinary landscape.

Philadelphia Food Truck Commissary
  • A Philadelphia food truck commissary is a facility that provides essential services and support for food truck operators. Commissaries serve as centralized locations where food truck owners can store, prepare, and maintain their mobile kitchens. These facilities offer a range of amenities and resources that help food truck businesses operate more efficiently and comply with health and safety regulations. They also offer trainings, workshops, networking and opportunities to collaborate for food truck owners.

How can a Food Truck Business Help its Community?
  • This is a blog post from Legion Food Trucks website detailing a number of ways a food truck owner can give back to its community. It includes several examples of food trucks in different cities and ways they supported their communities during the pandemic, as well as specific suggestions as to ways food truck owners can offer support to their own communities.

Feed America: Mobile Food Pantries
  • Feeding America's Mobile Food Pantries are a vital component of their effort to combat hunger and food insecurity across the United States. These mobile pantries operate as a direct-response strategy, bringing nutritious food and essential resources directly to communities in need, particularly in areas where access to traditional food assistance programs may be limited.

How This Local Food Truck Uses Bagels and Social Media to Support Nonprofits
  • Beyond its delicious offerings, Schmear It is known for its commitment to social impact. The food truck has a philanthropic component, aiming to support and raise awareness for various community initiatives. For instance, they have partnered with local nonprofits, and a portion of their sales often goes toward charitable causes, contributing to the betterment of the Philadelphia community.

Mobile Food News: Philadelphia
  • This is a news organization with up-to-date articles and information specifically related to mobile food organizations based in Philadelphia. It also contains pages with broader news related to the food truck business, and is a useful source of both local and country-wide industry information.
