Food Caterer License

You need this license to operate a food business in a permanent location where you prepare food to be sold off-site for public consumption.

Get a Food Caterer License (City of Philadelphia)

What to know at a high level:

  • License Requirement: Individuals and businesses intending to provide catering services in Philadelphia must obtain a Food Caterer License.
  • Application Process: Applications can be submitted online or in-person at the Municipal Services Building. A link to the online application portal is provided.
  • Required Documents: Applicants need to provide photo identification, proof of insurance, a copy of the Philadelphia Commercial Activity License, a menu of food and beverages, and a catering facility floor plan.
  • Fees and Renewal: There are fees associated with the license application and renewal. The Food Caterer License is valid for one year and requires annual renewal.
  • Compliance and Inspections: Caterers must adhere to health and safety regulations, undergo regular inspections by the Department of Public Health, and maintain proper food handling and sanitation standards.

Plan Review for a Mobile Food Business

A plan review is part of getting your mobile food business up and running. You should submit your plan review application to the Office of Food Protection before installing new food equipment or making any changes to an already approved mobile food vending unit. You may be charged an extra fee if you begin installation or make changes before your plan review has been approved.
